Marcol intends to become the first-choice supplier in plastics fabrication through its commitment to supply quality, defect-free products and services first time, every time, on the due date and at realistic prices.

The company ethos is if you have a problem, Marcol has the solution. With Marcol, you are in very good hands.

The Quality Policy for Marcol Fabrications has now been realigned to reflect the changes within the new Standard ISO9001:2015 and how these changes influence the business context and its strategic direction as well as embracing the requirements of both internal and external parties.

It is incumbent on Marcol Fabrications to identify all interested parties that influences the business and its context. Marcol has set out to recognise and determine levels of Risk that could impact the business and affect those applicable interested parties.

Having identified the Risks to the business Marcol Fabrications set out to plan and implement actions to address those Risks by either reducing or eliminating those Risks!

Business Objectives are set to encompass and address perceived issues that could adversely influence the business and also to fully support the achievement of the Quality Policy as well as the Quality Management System and to provide a platform for continual improvement.

Marcol continue to identify applicable legislation and strives to ensure compliance in all respects.

The Business has established a full CSR (Corporate Social Responsibility and Sustainability Policy) that emphasises its commitment to its employees (internal interested parties) as well as it external interested parties.

The business has established an active Internal Audit Program to monitor, measure and review the delivery and effectiveness of these plans.
