{% include section-header.html title="What We Do" %}

Marcol manufacture equipment designed to operate across the complete spectrum of environments; scrubbers for chemical plants or clean room equipment in laboratories are examples of the diverse range of applications we offer.

Our facilities at Bristol are purpose designed for the manufacture of large-scale equipment and we use the latest developments in welding and fusion technology. Our flexibility allows us to undertake short-run batch production for items such as containment trays and machine guarding.

All of our staff are fully trained and skilled in the various welding and fusion techniques used in modern plastics bonding and fabrication. We have the skills to fabricate complicated shapes such as square to round and complex curves. Our company policy promotes ongoing staff development and training as an essential investment.

From our intial contact we are here to help you. We are able to provide advice, and develop your ideas for you, based on our numerous years of experience. Whatever your requirements in plastics fabrication we can provide a solution to meet your needs. Our service includes design and advice, detail drawings and specification, manufacture and, where appropriate, installation— including “Turnkey Projects”. If required, our engineers will attend site and take measurements, collecting data for analysis and reporting.

We offer pre-tender meetings, full HAZOP discussions and are fully conversant with health and safety and other relevant legislation.

When you next require a well-specified, quality product delivered and/or installed safely, on time and within budget, please contact us.
